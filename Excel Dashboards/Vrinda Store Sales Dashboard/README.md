# Vrinda Store Sales Dashboard

## Overview

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing Vrinda Store's annual sales performance for 2022. The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, sales distribution, order status, regional performance, and sales channels to support business decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard](dashboard_preview.png)

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze annual sales performance
- Identify top-performing states
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Compare sales across different sales channels
- Monitor order fulfillment status

---

## KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Delivery Rate

---

## Dashboard Features

- Monthly Sales vs Orders Trend
- Sales Distribution by Gender
- Order Status Analysis
- Top Performing States
- Orders by Age & Gender
- Sales Channel Distribution

## Key Insights

- Women accounted for the majority of total sales.
- Maharashtra generated the highest revenue among all states.
- Amazon was the leading sales channel.
- Over 90% of orders were successfully delivered.
- Adult customers represented the largest purchasing segment.
