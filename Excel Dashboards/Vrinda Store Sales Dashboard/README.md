# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard

![Dashboard Preview](vrinda_store.png)

## Project Overview

This Excel dashboard provides an interactive analysis of Vrinda Store's annual sales performance for the year 2022. It presents key business metrics related to sales, customer demographics, order status, regional performance, and sales channels, enabling stakeholders to make informed business decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze monthly sales and order trends.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior by gender and age group.
- Evaluate order fulfillment performance.
- Identify top-performing states based on sales.
- Compare order distribution across different online sales channels.

---

## Dashboard Features

- Monthly Orders vs Sales Trend
- Sales Distribution by Gender
- Order Status Analysis
- Top 5 States by Sales
- Orders by Age Group & Gender
- Orders by Sales Channel

##  Key Insights

- Women contributed **64%** of total sales, making them the primary customer segment.
- Over **92%** of all orders were successfully delivered.
- **Maharashtra** generated the highest sales among all states.
- **Amazon** was the leading sales channel, followed by Myntra and Flipkart.
- Adult customers placed the highest number of orders compared to teenagers and seniors.
